HOW TO PLAY

⚔️

A random Pokémon appears on the left. You must find a Pokémon that beats it in the shown stat category.

🏆

If your Pokémon wins, it becomes the new defender next round. Keep winning to build your streak!

💀

If you lose, the enemy Pokémon stays and defends again. Your streak resets to 0.

🚫

No Pokémon can be used twice. Once a Pokémon has fought, it disappears from the search list forever.

🔥

Goal: get the highest streak possible before running out of Pokémon or losing.
